Ignoring every channels join/part/quit messages in IRSSI

Date Sat 11 May 2013 Tags irssi / linux

Tired of all those join part messages?

/ignore \* joins  
/ignore \* parts  
/ignore \* quits  
/save

Read more about ignore /help ignore
